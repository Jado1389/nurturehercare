What We Offer

Services Crafted for New Mothers

From gentle home care to personal wellness — every service is designed to give you the rest and support you deserve after birth.

🏠

Homecare Support for New Mothers

Comprehensive in-home support tailored to your postnatal needs. We provide attentive, compassionate care so you can focus on healing, bonding, and settling into motherhood with confidence.

🧹

Cleaning Services

We offer Standard and Deep cleaning options. From tidying up living areas to thorough sanitizing, choose the level of clean that suits your family — and we'll handle the rest with care and professionalism.

💤

Mama Break

You deserve rest. We take care of your baby while you enjoy a nap, a warm bath, or simply a quiet moment to yourself. Recharging is not a luxury — it's a necessity, and we're here for it.

👕

Folding Clothes & Laundry

We'll handle your laundry pile with love — folding tiny baby onesies and cozy mama sweaters alike, leaving everything neat, organized, and easy to find. One less thing on your mind.

🛍️

Running Errands

Grocery shopping, pharmacy pick-ups, post-office drop-offs — tell us what you need, and we'll go. Save your energy for what matters most: you and your baby.

🚗

Nurture Her Rides

We'll safely drive you to appointments and bring you back home. Designed for new and postpartum moms, our rides remove the stress of navigating transport during this precious time.

💆

In-Home Massage

Bring the spa to you. Our professional massage therapist delivers a soothing, restorative experience right in the comfort of your home — because you deserve to feel your best without stepping out.

🌿

Need Something Else?

Every mother's journey is unique. If you need a service not listed here, reach out — we'll do our best to tailor our care to your specific situation and make it happen.

About Us

We Believe Every Mother Deserves to Be Cared For

Nurture Her Care was born from a simple but powerful belief: the postnatal period is one of the most demanding transitions in a woman's life — and no mother should navigate it alone.

Our team of compassionate caregivers brings warmth, professionalism, and genuine care to every home we visit. We partner with new mothers across Calgary to ease the everyday burdens so they can heal, bond, and thrive.

Our Mission

To help new mothers navigate their postnatal journey by offering a range of carefully designed services that ease the transition into motherhood — physically, emotionally, and practically.
